OUR JOURNEY

From a humble garage project to a defining voice in streetwear culture. This is the story of FYNEST.

2020

The Inception

Founded in a small garage in Bandung with a single sewing machine and a vision to redefine local streetwear.

2021

First Collection

Released "Genesis Drop" which sold out in 48 hours, marking our entry into the national market.

2022

Expansion

Opened our flagship store in Jakarta and partnered with international fabric suppliers for premium quality.

2023

Going Global

Started shipping worldwide and collaborated with top urban artists for limited edition attributes.

2024

FYNEST Era

Rebranded to FYNEST to reflect our commitment to being the absolute best in the game.

VISION & MISSION

VISION

To become the global standard for premium, accessible streetwear that empowers individual expression.

MISSION

1. Deliver uncompromising quality in every stitch.
2. Foster a community of creators and innovators.
3. Sustainable production practices.
