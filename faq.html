FREQUENTLY ASKED

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 24 hours. Standard shipping takes 2-3 business days within Java and 4-7 days for other regions.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the item is unworn and tags are attached. Please contact support to initiate a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to selected countries in Southeast Asia (Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Philippines).

How do I track my order?

Once shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email/WhatsApp which you can check on our courier partner's website.
